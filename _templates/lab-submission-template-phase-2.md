PKI Foundations Lab Templates

These templates help you structure your lab submissions and notes.

How to use:
1. Open the template you need.
2. Click "Raw".
3. Copy the content.
4. Paste it into a new .md file in your portfolio repository.

Templates available:
- Lab Instructions Template
- Lesson Notes Template
- Reflection Template
- Week README Template

## Phase 2 Note

**Phase 2 labs (Week 9 onward) do not use the generic templates above.** Each week has its own lab submission template located directly in the week folder:

```
week-09/labs/lab-01-environment-verification.md
week-09/labs/lab-02-environment-documentation.md
```

The pattern is `week-##/labs/lab-##-<name>.md`. Copy the relevant file into your portfolio repo under `labs/week-##/` before filling it in.

The templates in `_templates/` are for Phase 1 use only.
